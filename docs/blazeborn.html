Blazeborn

Blazeborns are thought to be Blaze-Human hybrids who have retained their love for fire but lost their ability to shoot it. Their prime environment is in the fiery depths of the Nether, but most of their time is spent swimming in lava since they do not remember how to fly. Blazeborns face an interesting but great challenge early on: getting to the Overworld.
