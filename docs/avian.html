Avian

Avians are a race of humanoid birds known for their vegetarian lifestyle. Like chickens, their wings are not strong enough for flight, but they are strong enough to decrease their falling speed as if they experience half the gravity of other creatures. They are aerodynamic, leading to faster movement, and they still lay eggs like their ancestors.
